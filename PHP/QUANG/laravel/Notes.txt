Vào trang https://getcomposer.org/download/
-> tải composer
-> check composer install composer --version (nhớ reload vscode)

## Tạo dự án
composer create-project laravel/laravel hellolaravel
composer create-project --prefer-dist laravel/laravel hellolaravel
--> CD tới folde dự án: php artisan serve

## Tạo controller
php artisan make:controller ProductController
